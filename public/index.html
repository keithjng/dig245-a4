What is wireframing?

  • Wireframing is the bare structure/design of a webpage
  • It's essentially a blueprint for what a webpage should look like

Why use wireframing?

  • Wireframes typically lack color or specific design elements.
  • They allow creators to just focus on the spacing of a webpage
  • Applications allow designers to create multiple renditions of the same product
  • Certain wireframe applications have mobile browser templates
